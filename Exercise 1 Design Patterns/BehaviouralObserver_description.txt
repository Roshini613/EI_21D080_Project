This Java program showcases a vibrant stock market simulation using the Observer design pattern. 
In this setup, Investor and Analyst classes eagerly track market shifts through the StockMarket subject.
As stock prices dance and fluctuate, the StockMarket ensures that all registered observers are promptly updated with real-time information.
Users can effortlessly add investors and analysts, adjust stock prices, and witness immediate notifications. 
This engaging implementation vividly demonstrates how the Observer pattern can craft dynamic, interactive systems that respond fluidly to changes, making stock market interactions both thrilling and informative.
